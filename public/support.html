❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Download the TeeMates app from the App Store or Google Play. Tap "Get Started" and sign up with your email or Google account. Complete your golf profile to start matching with other players.
How does the matching system work?
TeeMates matches you based on your playing preferences, skill level, availability, and location. You can browse tee times posted by other players and request to join, or create your own tee time and let others find you.
How do I book a tee time?
Browse available tee times on the home feed and request to join one that fits your schedule. Or post your own tee time specifying the course, date, time, and how many spots are available. Once your squad is complete, coordinate via the in-app chat.
Can I cancel or leave a tee time?
Yes, you can leave a tee time from the booking details page. Please try to give as much notice as possible so other players can find a replacement. Frequent last-minute cancellations may affect your reputation in the community.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove your profile and all associated data. This action cannot be undone. You can also email us at support@teemates.ca to request deletion.
How do I report a problem or user?
Tap the menu icon on any user profile or tee time and select "Report". Provide details about the issue. Reports are reviewed by our community tribunal system. You can also email support@teemates.ca for urgent issues.
